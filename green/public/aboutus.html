About Green-Articles

Our Story


Welcome green-articles,A Shop from Nagpur, city of orange where nature meets style. Our journey began with a passion for bringing the beauty of plants into homes, transforming spaces into green sanctuaries.

Founded in 1999, we curate a collection of exquisite plants and uniquely crafted pots to elevate your home decor. Each plant is handpicked for its quality and aesthetics, and every pot is chosen to complement the natural beauty of the green world.

Meet our Team

We are a team of passionate individuals dedicated to bringing the best of the plant world to your doorstep. From plant enthusiasts to design experts, our diverse team collaborates to create a curated selection that suits every taste and style.
